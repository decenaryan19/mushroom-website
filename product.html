Fresh Organic Oak Log Shiitake Mushrooms

Our shiitake mushrooms are grown using the traditional Japanese method of log cultivation — a time-honored practice that produces mushrooms of exceptional quality, flavor, and nutritional value. Each mushroom emerges from sustainably harvested oak logs placed in our forest shade house, where natural humidity and temperature create the perfect growing conditions.

Unveil the Essence of Shiitake Mushrooms

Shiitake mushrooms (Lentinula edodes) have been prized for centuries in Asian cuisine for their rich, savory umami flavor and meaty texture. Our forest-grown shiitakes develop a distinctive cracked cap pattern — a sign of high-quality "donko" mushrooms that connoisseurs seek out for their concentrated flavor.

Nurtured by Nature's Embrace

Unlike commercially grown mushrooms on synthetic substrates, our shiitakes grow slowly on whole oak logs over 8–12 months. This natural process allows the mycelium to fully colonize the wood, drawing nutrients that translate into superior taste and texture. No pesticides, no artificial growing mediums — just oak, water, and forest air.

A Masterpiece of Flavors and Nutrients

Our shiitake mushrooms are naturally rich in:

  • Vitamin D (especially when sun-exposed before harvest)
  • B vitamins (B2, B3, B5, B6, and B9)
  • Copper, selenium, and zinc
  • Beta-glucans and lentinan polysaccharides
  • Dietary fiber and plant-based protein

Embark on a Culinary Adventure

Whether you're grilling them with a simple brush of olive oil and sea salt, roasting them until caramelized and crispy, or adding them to a vibrant stir-fry, our shiitake mushrooms elevate every dish. Their meaty texture makes them an excellent plant-based alternative in burgers, tacos, and pasta dishes.
